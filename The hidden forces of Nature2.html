The hinges of love

Everything seemed magical until Justin took the drinking and partying to the next level, causing fractures in their relationship. He remembers breaking up at least 7 times during their time together. On the months leading up to their last summer, Justin told Kate he was heading into rehab and that it would be best if she took some time away from him.

What did Kate do next?


  • Kate stayed by Justin's side and together they built one of the biggest rehab franchises in the US

  • Kate transferred into an all-girls school and then moved to London

  • Justin sobered up and went on to become one of the most famous singers of all time
